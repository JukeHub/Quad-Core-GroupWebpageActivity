Team Rules and Values

  • Open Communication: We will clearly communicate our schedules and emphasize any potential roadblocks while completing tasks.
  • Reliability: We will commit to completing our assigned tasks in a timely manner, so no one is left waiting on other members.
  • Equal Contribution: When dividing the workload, we will ensure that everyone has a relatively equal number of tasks.
